The Problem

Most people don't know which AI model to use.

  • Defaulting to GPT-4 for everything (expensive, slow for simple tasks)
  • Randomly picking Claude because they heard it's good
  • Zero visibility into cost vs quality vs speed tradeoffs
  • Wasting 30-40% on models they don't actually need
  • No easy way to compare which model actually performs best

The Solution

ModelRouter is a dashboard where you describe your problem, we engineer a better prompt, find the right models (paid + free, global), and show you side-by-side results.

Type your problem (raw, unfiltered)
Answer 1-3 clarifying questions
Review & approve engineered prompt
Select models to compare
See results: cost, speed, quality
